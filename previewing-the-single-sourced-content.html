Preview the single-sourced content

When you preview the topic with reused content, Writerside displays it to look for the end-user: it exposes all includes, inlines all variables, and applies all filters. Therefore, you see the final version of the topic with all the content in place.

Also, you can navigate from the source of the included piece using Ctrl+Click on the element-id value of the include element.
