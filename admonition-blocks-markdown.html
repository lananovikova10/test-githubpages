Admonition blocks

Admonitions are formatted text blocks used to highlight important information, warn users, or provide tips. They are colored and have an icon to the left according to the block type.

By default, the quote markup is rendered as a tip. But you can alter it using the style attribute placed in the curly brackets after the block.

Markup

Result

> This is > a tip

This is a tip.

Markup

Result

> Just FYI. > {style="note"}

Just FYI.

Markup

Result

> You shall > not pass > {style="warning"}

You shall not pass.
