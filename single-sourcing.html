Single sourcing and content reuse

Writerside provides rich functionality for single sourcing and content reuse.

Single sourcing the content means writing it once and then using in different contexts instead of copy-pasting.

You can reuse the topics, chapters, paragraphs, sentences, and tree sections and customize them using filters and variables.
